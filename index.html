Our Menu

Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is often served with fries or a side salad and can be customized with various toppings, sauces, and seasonings to suit individual preferences.

Beef Biryani

Beef Biryani is often served with cooling side dishes such as raita (a yogurt-based side dish with cucumber and spices) or salad. It is also commonly enjoyed with boiled eggs and pickles, which add extra flavor and contrast.

Tikka Boti

Tikka Boti is commonly served with raita, naan or paratha for scooping, and a fresh salad. Chutney and pickles add extra flavor. Lemon wedges and onion rings provide a refreshing and crunchy contrast

Sik Kabab

Sik Kebab is served with naan or paratha, perfect for scooping up the flavorful meat. It is accompanied by raita for a cooling effect and a fresh salad with tomatoes, onions, and cucumbers. Chutney and pickles add an extra tangy kick.

Tandoori chicken kebab

Tandoori Chicken is typically served with raita, naan or paratha to complement the smoky flavors. A fresh salad of cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions adds crunch. Lemon wedges and pickles enhance the taste with a tangy and spicy kick.

Russian Salad

Russian Salad is a creamy, tangy salad made with a variety of mixed vegetables and fruits, typically bound together with mayonnaise or a creamy dressing. The dish is popular in many countries and often served as a side dish or appetizer. The main ingredients include boiled potatoes, carrots, peas, and apples, along with additional vegetables like corn, beans, or cucumber. Sometimes, fruits such as pineapple or grapes are also added for sweetness. The salad is seasoned with salt, pepper, and a dash of lemon juice, making it a refreshing and rich side dish perfect for any occasion.

Mix Mithai

The mix is often garnished with chopped nuts like almonds, cashews, or pistachios, adding a crunchy texture and extra flavor.
